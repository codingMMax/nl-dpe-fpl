=== Latency Alignment (tb_dimm_pipeline_latency.v) ===

Comparison unit: per DPE pass (= 1 score pair for K_id=2).
NOT amortized per-score — the DPE pass is the actual observable unit.

IMC Simulator (per DPE pass):
  read     = ceil(128×8/40) = 26 cycles
  compute  = 3 cycles (ACAM)
  output   = ceil(128×8/40) = 26 cycles
  CLB_add  = 1 cycle
  CLB_reduce = ceil(log2(64)) = 6 cycles
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  per_pass = 1 + (26+3+26) + 6 = 62 cycles

RTL Measured (per DPE pass):
  Score pair [0,1]: completed at cycle 66-67 (64 cycles from start)
  Score pair [2,3]: completed at cycle 131-132 (65 cycles from pair 0)
  Per DPE pass: ~65 cycles

Delta: +3 cycles/pass (FSM overhead)
  - 2 cycles: SRAM read latency (address register + registered read)
  - 1 cycle: drain wait + score write

Stage-by-Stage:
  | Stage        | Simulator | RTL    | Match |
  |-------------|-----------|--------|-------|
  | SRAM→DPE    | 26 cyc    | 26 cyc | ✓     |
  | DPE compute | 3 cyc     | 3+4 cyc| +4    |
  | DPE output  | 26 cyc    | 26 cyc | ✓     |
  | Per pass    | 62 cyc    | 65 cyc | +3    |

Note: The +4 controller overhead on DPE compute is partially
absorbed by pipeline overlap with the CLB add/reduce stages,
resulting in only +3 net overhead per full pass.

DPE Statistics:

  dpe_done pulses: 52 total (26 per pass × 2 passes) ✓
